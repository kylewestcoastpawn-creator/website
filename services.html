Our Services

Everything you need — under one roof

Buy Firearms

We've got handguns, rifles, and shotguns — new and used. Inventory changes all the time, so stop in and see what's on the wall.

We Buy Collections

Looking to sell a whole collection? We'll buy it all — big or small. Bring it in or give us a call and we can come to you.

Sell Your Guns

Got a gun you want to sell? Bring it in. We'll take a look, give you a fair price, and pay you cash right there.

Trade-Ins

Ready for something different? Trade in what you've got and put the value toward your next one. Simple as that.

Pawn Loans

Need quick cash? Use your firearms or anything of value as collateral for a short-term pawn loan. Discreet, fast, and confidential.

FFL Transfers

Bought a gun online? Ship it to us and we'll handle the paperwork. $40 per firearm, $10 for each additional on the same background check, and $100 for NFA transfers.

Accessories & Ammo

Ammo, optics, holsters, mags, cleaning kits — we carry all the stuff you need to go with your firearm.

NFA Items

Silencers, short-barreled rifles, and more. The paperwork can be a headache — we'll walk you through the whole thing.

We Buy Precious Metals

Got gold, silver, or platinum sitting around? Bring it in — we'll weigh it, test it, and give you a fair cash offer on the spot.

Layaway

Don't want to pay all at once? No problem. Put 25% down and pay it off monthly for up to 4 months. Works on any item in the store.
