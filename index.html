Store Policies

We operate by the book — always

🪪 Background Checks

All firearm purchases require a completed ATF Form 4473 and a federal NICS background check. No exceptions. We follow all federal and state laws regarding firearm transfers.

📋 Valid ID Required

A valid government-issued photo ID is required for all purchases, pawn transactions, and FFL transfers. You must meet all legal age requirements for the firearm you wish to purchase.

🔄 Returns & Exchanges

All sales are final on used firearms. New firearms may be exchanged within 7 days if unfired and in original condition. We stand behind everything we sell.

🏦 Pawn Loan Policy

Pawn loans are short-term collateral loans. Items not redeemed within the loan period become property of the store. Loan terms and interest rates are clearly disclosed at time of transaction.
